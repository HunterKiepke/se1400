Target Audience Analysis

Primary Demographics

Geolocation - Where Our Audience Lives

Our primary audience will be concentrated in major football markets across the United States, with particular strength in:

  • NFL Markets: Dallas-Fort Worth, New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Philadelphia, Boston, and other major NFL cities
  • College Football Hotbeds: Southeastern states (Alabama, Georgia, Florida, Louisiana), Texas, Ohio, Michigan, and California
  • Secondary Markets: Midwestern states with strong college football traditions and smaller NFL markets

Age - Primary Age Ranges

Our target audience spans multiple generations but focuses on these key age groups:

  • 18-24: College students and young adults who are active on social media and consume content primarily through mobile devices
  • 25-34: Young professionals who are deeply engaged with fantasy football and have disposable income for premium content
  • 35-44: Established professionals who are passionate about football and seek in-depth analysis and historical context
  • 45-54: Experienced fans who appreciate traditional sports journalism and have strong brand loyalty
  • 55+: Long-time football enthusiasts who value comprehensive coverage and community engagement

Gender - Target Demographics

While football has traditionally been male-dominated, our audience strategy includes:

  • Primary: Male audience (approximately 65-70% of users) who are the traditional core of football fandom
  • Growing Segment: Female audience (approximately 30-35% of users) who represent the fastest-growing demographic in football viewership
  • Inclusive Approach: Content and features designed to appeal to all football fans regardless of gender

Additional Demographics

Education Level

Our audience includes a wide range of educational backgrounds:

  • College-educated professionals who appreciate analytical content and detailed statistics
  • High school graduates who are passionate about their local teams and community
  • Graduate degree holders who seek sophisticated analysis and expert commentary

Income Level

Target income demographics include:

  • Middle Class ($40,000-$100,000): Core audience with disposable income for premium content and merchandise
  • Upper Middle Class ($100,000-$200,000): High-value users who can afford premium subscriptions and exclusive content
  • Students and Young Professionals: Lower income but high engagement potential and future earning power

Technology Usage

Our audience is tech-savvy and multi-platform:

  • Mobile-First: 70% of content consumption happens on smartphones and tablets
  • Social Media Active: High engagement on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok
  • Streaming Comfortable: Familiar with digital content consumption and willing to pay for quality

Football Engagement Level

We target multiple engagement levels:

  • Casual Fans: Watch games occasionally and want basic information and highlights
  • Regular Fans: Follow their teams closely and consume content weekly
  • Hardcore Fans: Daily engagement, fantasy football players, and statistical analysis enthusiasts
  • Industry Professionals: Coaches, players, journalists, and analysts who need detailed insights

Interests and Behaviors

Our audience shares common interests beyond football:

  • Fantasy sports participation and strategy
  • Sports betting and gambling (where legal)
  • Video games and gaming culture
  • Social media and content sharing
  • Community and social engagement
  • Technology and innovation
